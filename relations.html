Template 1: VRMs with customer visit preferences

This template is for VRMs where each customer submits a request for visiting a given location. Request cases are requesting a repair, a pick up of a parcel, a pick up of a person, a delivery of a parcel, and a drop-off of a person. The template can be used for modelling the following existing problems: Capacitated VRP (CVRP); CVRP with Multiple Depots; CVRP with Split Drop-offs; CVRP with Backhauls; CVRP with Pickups or Drop-offs; CVRP with Time Windows. Check the Neo and CVRPLIB websites for these models. However, the template is strictly more general. For example, vehicles can have different capacities, velocities, costs, and emissions. Drop-offs can start from different depots and pickups can end at different depots. Requests can have arbitrary costs. Each location has an associated service time.

  • PROBLEM SIZE
  • [number of drivers N];[number of customers M];
  • FLEET
  • [vehicle1];[velocity1];[capacity1];[cost1];[emissions1];
  • ...
  • [vehicleN];[velocityN];[capacityN];[costN];[emissionsN];
  • DRIVER PREFERENCES
  • [vehicle1];[start1 service_s1 time_window_s1];[end1 service_e1 time_window_e1];
  • ...
  • [vehicle_N];[startN service_sN time_window_sN];[endN service_eN time_window_eN];
  • CUSTOMER PREFERENCES
  • [request1];[loc1 service1 time_window1 demand1 cost1];
  • ...
  • [requestM];[locM serviceM time_windowM demandM costM];

Template 2: VRMs with customer pickup and dropoff preferences

his template is for VRMs where each customer submits a pickup and dropoff request. Two common request cases are collecting and shipping a parcel, as well as pickuping up and dropping off a person. The template can be used for modelling the following problems: Capacitated Pick and Drop-offs Problem (CPDP); CPDP with Multiple Depots; CPDP with Split Drop-offs; CPDP with Headhauls; CPDP with Time Windows. For example, vehicles can have different capacities, velocities, costs, emissions, and current depot locations. All pickups can be serviced before all dropoffs. Pickups can be at different locations and drop-offs can also be at different locations. Requests can have arbitrary costs. Each location has an associated service time.

  • PROBLEM SIZE
  • [number of drivers N];[number of customers M];
  • FLEET
  • [vehicle1];[velocity1];[capacity1];[cost1];[emissions1];
  • ...
  • [vehicleN];[velocityN];[capacityN];[costN];[emissionsN];
  • DRIVER PREFERENCES
  • [vehicle1];[start1 service_s1 time_window_s1];[end1 service_e1 time_window_e1];
  • ...
  • [vehicleN];[startN service_sN time_window_sN];[endN service_eN time_window_eN];
  • CUSTOMER PREFERENCES
  • [request1];[pickup1 service_p1 time_window_p1 demand_p1 cost_p1];[dropoff1 service_d1 time_window_d1 demand_d1 cost_d1];
  • ...
  • [requestM];[pickupM service_pM time_window_pM demand_pM cost_pM];[dropoffM service_dM time_window_dM demand_dM cost_dM];

Template 3: VRMs with driver or customer transport preferences

This template is for VRMs where each user (i.e. driver/customer) submits a transport request. Two request cases are charging at the nearest e-station and sightseeing through popular locations. The template can be used for modelling the following problems: VRP with Satellite Facilities; VRP with Charging Preferences; VRP with Commuting Preferences; VRP with Sightseeing Preferences; VRP with Healthcare Preferences. For example, drivers may have preferences about where to re-fill their vehicle. As a second example, carers may also have preferences in which order to visit their patients. As a third example, commuters may have preferences about which route to take on the way to/from work. Finally, tourists may prefer to tour through specific popular places.

  • PROBLEM SIZE
  • [number of drivers N];[number of customers M];
  • FLEET
  • [vehicle1];[velocity1];[capacity1];[cost1];[emissions1];
  • ...
  • [vehicleN];[velocityN];[capacityN];[costN];[emissionsN];
  • DRIVER PREFERENCES
  • [vehicle1];[vloc11 service11 time_window11 demand11];...;[vloc1n_1 service1n_1 time_window1n_1 demand1n_1];
  • ...
  • [vehicleN];[vlocN1 serviceN1 time_windowN1 demandN1];...;[vlocNn_N serviceNn_N time_windowNn_N demandNn_N];
  • CUSTOMER PREFERENCES
  • [request1];[rloc11 service11 time_window11 demand11 cost11];...;[rloc1k_1 service1k_1 time_window1k_1 demand1k_1 cost1k_1];
  • ...
  • [requestM];[rlocM1 serviceM1 time_windowM1 demandM1 costM1];...;[rlocMk_M serviceMk_M time_windowMk_M demandMk_M costMk_M];
