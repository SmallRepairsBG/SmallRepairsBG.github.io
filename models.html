Capacitated Vehicle Routing Models (VRMs) with Preferences

Capacitated Vehicle Routing Model (CVRM)

Our CVRM has a fleet of vehicles and a number of requests for visits at customer locations. All vehicles start and end at the same depot location. CVRM allows for modelling the following features: vehicles' available capacities, operation costs, gas emissions, and safety levels; requests' services times; eligibility ralitions between drivers and customers, driver (profit) rankings over customers, and customer (cost) rankings over drivers.

CVRM with Pickups (CVRMP)

CVRMP is our CVRM plus a number of requests for pickups at customer locations. Hence, it generalizes our CVRM. The requests could be for bringing parcels or people to the depot location. For example, this sould be a hub or a port. CVRMP allows for modelling possibly distinct supplies at the pickup locations.

CVRM with Drop-offs (CVRMD)

CVRMD is our CVRM plus a number of requests for drop-offs at customer locations. Hence, it generalizes our CVRM. The drop-offs could be for bringing parcels or people from the depot location. For example, this could be a hub or a port. CVRMD allows for modelling possibly distinct demands at the drop-off locations.

CVRM with Pickups and Drop-offs (CVRMPD)

CVRMPD is our CVRM plus a number of requests for pickups and a number of requests for drop-offs at customer locations. Hence, it generalizes our CVRMP and CVRMD. The pickups and drop-offs could be for bringing parcels or people respectively to and from the depot location. CVRMPD allows for modelling possibly distinct supplies at the pickup locations and demands at the drop-off locations.

CVRMPD with Multiple Depots (CVRMPDMD)

CVRMPDMD is our CVRMPD in which vehicles can start from possibly distinct depot locations and end at other possible distinct depot locations. Hence, it generalizes our CVRMPD. The depot locations could be drivers' home locations or work locations.

Capacitated Vehicle Scheduling Models (VSMs) with Preferences

CVRMPDMD with Time Windows (CVRMPDMDTW)

CVRMPDMDTW is our CVRMPDMD in which vehicles have multiple operation windows and customers have multiple time windows. Hence, it spoils down to our CVRMPD in case the windows are open wide enough. The presence of operation and time windows restricts the orders in which the vehicles visit the customers and, as a result, scheduling must takes place about when the drivers should visit their locations.

CVRMPDMD with Shift Windows (CVRMPDMDSW)

CVRMPDMDSW is our CVRMPDMD in which vehicles have multiple operation windows and drivers have multiple shift windows. Hence, it spoils down to our CVRMPD in case the windows are open wide enough. The presence of operation and shift windows restricts the orders in which the vehicles visit the customers and, as a result, scheduling must takes place about when the drivers should visit their locations.
