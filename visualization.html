Plots

Visualize the solution, based on custom preferences.

We present the solutions to VRP Problems in form of plots and graphs for ease of understanding. You can choose a dataset and see the solution for it. You may also provide your own dataset on Vehicle Routes, Travel Time, Available Capacity and Location Co-ordinates. It is also possible to choose which vehicle details are to be shown, among all possible options.

You may also customize the plots in the following ways:

1. Check Travel Time

You can see how much time each vehicle takes for travelling from point A to B. The thickness of lines in the connected graph between two points are proportionate to the travel time, for that particular vehicle.

2. Check Available Capacity

You can see what is the available capacity for each vehicle from point A to B. The size of the markers in the connected graph are proportionate to the available capacity at that location, for that particular vehicle.

3. Check Both Travel Time and Available Capacity

You can also see both the options together, for each vehicle, at each location.
